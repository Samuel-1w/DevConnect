About DevConnect

A community build for developers who are serious about growing their craft.

💡

Our Mission

DevConnect exists to give developers a focused, distraction-free space to share knowledge, celebrate wins, ask questions, and build real professional connections.

👥

Who Is It For?

Whether you are a student writing your first line of code or a senior engineer with years of experience, DevConnect is built for anyone passionate about software development.

🔒

Privacy First

Your data stays on your device. DevConnect uses browser local storage - no accounts are sent to external servers, no data is sold, no tracking.

⚙️

Our Stack

  • HTML5 and CSS3 for structure and style
  • Vanilla JavaScript for logic and interactivity
  • jQuery for dynamic UI effects
  • Browser localStorage for data persistance
